Rasomati's Story

Rasomati Sarkar lives in Tehsil-Kishanganj in the Baran District of Rajasthan. She learned about computers through the 2E2 Programme and now she is teaching her new-found skills to the other women in her community.

However, she is not just a trainer, she is also a campaigner; promoting the importance of computer education and digitalization. She also helps members of her community to fill out forms to apply for their old-age pension, to apply for their Adhaar number (necessary for the Indian biometric ID system), to carry out net banking, make ticket bookings and make ‘Right to information’ applications. She also gives information to community members about Government schemes.
